About Us

Karsazian Welfare Association is a charitable and non-profit organization founded by the students of Karsazian School in Karachi. Our mission is to build an educated and healthy Pakistan by providing sustainable support to underserved communities.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to improving education and healthcare in rural areas through sustainable initiatives. Initially providing medical assistance, assistance in natural calamities, medical camps, and assistance in educational matters, we have expanded our reach to serve over 500 families across 12 rural projects.
