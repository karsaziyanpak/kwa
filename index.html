About Us

Karsazian Welfare Association is a charitable and non-profit organization founded by students of Karsazian School in Karachi. Our mission is to build an educated and healthy Pakistan by providing sustainable support to underserved communities.

Our Mission

We improve education and healthcare in rural areas through sustainable initiatives, serving over 500 families across 12 rural projects.
