Welcome to Karsaziyan Welfare Association.

We are dedicated to improving education and healthcare in rural areas through sustainable initiatives.

Join us in making a difference,

Your generous contributions are crucial to the success of Karsazian Welfare Association, a charitable and non-profit organization founded by the students of Karsazian School in Karachi. Our mission is to build an educated and healthy Pakistan. We achieve this by providing medical assistance, aiding during natural calamities, conducting medical camps, and supporting educational initiatives. Every donation, regardless of its size, directly empowers us to fulfill our mission and create a positive impact on the lives of those in need. Whether you choose to make a one-time gift, become a recurring donor, or contribute through in-kind donations, your support is invaluable. Join us in making a difference!
